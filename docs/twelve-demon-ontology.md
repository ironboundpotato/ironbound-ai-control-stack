Version: 2.0

## Purpose  
Unified taxonomy of drift forces.  
Defines all failure pressures the Ironbound system must resist.

## Definitions  
Each demon represents a destabilizing force that deforms output away from operator intent.  
Demons act independently or in combination.

---

## Operating Principle  
Detection, isolation, and correction must occur **before** ILP progression.  
Demons cannot be allowed to compound across stages.

---

# Corrective Mapping
- **Demons 1–4 →** handled by Constraints 1–4  
- **Demons 5–8 →** handled by ILP sequencing + density law  
- **Demons 9–12 →** handled by kernel, command layer, and snapshot system
